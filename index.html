Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga is generally practiced slowly and with static posture holds than found in other styles. Hatha yoga has become associated solely with a physical practice, typically involving a sequence of asana (yoga postures) and pranayama (breathing techniques).

Iyengar Yoga

Iyengar yoga stemmed from the Hatha yoga tradition. Iyengar yoga is a disciplined system with carefully planned and timed sequences. Often, props, such as blocks and belts, are used to ensure students can practice the postures with correct alignment and safely develop their yoga practice over time.

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini yoga is also called the "yoga of awareness." The primary aim of Kundalini yoga is to awaken the kundalini energy, which lies coiled, serpent-like, at the base of the spine. In Kundalini yoga, practitioners use breathing, physical postures, chanting and meditation to unlock this energy.

Guided Meditation

Guided meditations are usually well-structured, and follow a relatively simple step-by-step format. Generally, teachers will explain a little about how the mind works, and how to calm it, before leading you through the sequential steps of a specific meditation technique.

Chakra Meditation

Chakra meditation is a form of meditation intended to activate, open and balance the seven chakras. Chakra meditation can be practiced to improve general well-being or to address specific issues with one or more chakras that are thought to be blocked or out of alignment.

Qigong Meditation

Qigong is a traditional Chinese system of postures, movement and meditation that is practiced to promote health, well-being and spiritual development. Like many types of yoga, it has an emphasis on coordinating breath with movement , but it is actually more closely related to Tai Chi.

Who We Are

Ana Cassidy

Ana is a Clare native, hailing from Crusheen. Having lived in Spain for many years with her partner, Marcus, they returned to her home with a vision to open a yoga studio and so 'Buddhi Yoga Studio' was born. Ana has significant training in the Hatha Yoga tradition over the past 20+ years, to include Iyengar Yoga. Ana also presents our Guided and Chakra Meditation classes.

Marcus Gonzalez

A native of Bilboa, in the basque region of Spain, Marcus relocated to Ireland with his partner Ana in May 2019 and made his home in her home town of Crusheen, Co. Clare. From here 'Buddhi Yoga Studio' was born. Marcus has almost 25 years' experience in the Hatha Yoga tradition, to include Iyengar Yoga. Marcus also presents our Qigong Meditation classes.

Aaliyah O'Halloran

Aaliyah has recently left the bright lights of her home city, Dublin, and relocated to Clare for a quieter pace of life. Aaliyah brings vast experience in the Kundalini Yoga tradition to the studio, with 16 years experience, 10 of which were spent teacher training at an ashram in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, India.
